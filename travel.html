My World: Culture & Travel

I view being Lebanese as the main thing that makes me myself. I value my culture because it changed my perspective on life and how I choose to live it. A popular saying is that Lebanon is a country of contradictions and contrasts: from the different views on every street to the different religions ands races and types of people you see everywhere. These things made me very open minded and gave me an appreciation for identity and belonging that I carry with me everywhere. More importantly, they gave me a curiosity; if my own culture is this rich, what else is out there? That question is what drives me to travel.

Every place I visit teaches me something new. I am drawn to the way culture shows up in small, everyday moments and interactions. Travel has made me more more adaptable, and very grateful. I hope to keep exploring for as long as I can.
