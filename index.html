Hi, I'm Noura!

My name is Noura, and if I had to describe myself in one sentence, I would say I am someone who has always been curious about the world and the people in it. I was born and raised in Lebanon, a small country with one of the richest cultures and histories in the entire world. Growing up Lebanese shaped everything about who I am, the way I connect with people, the importance I place on family and community and the deep appreciation I have for nature. It is a country that has faced immense hardship but remains one of the most vibrant and resilient places I have ever known. It also keps me connected to my family which is the most thing I value especially after moving very far away from them.

My love for culture does not stop at my own. One of my favorite things in life is traveling and discovering new places, new people, and new ways of living. I feel that experiencing other cultures is one of the most rewarding things a person can do. It teaches empathy, broadens perspective, and reminds you that there are so many beautiful ways to exist in this world. I speak English, Arabic, and French, which has opened so many doors for me when traveling and connecting with people from different backgrounds. I am currently studying at Northeastern University in Boston, but no matter where I am, I carry my culture with me everywhere I go.
